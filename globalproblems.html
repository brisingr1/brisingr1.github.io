Resource for Human Trafficking:

People Against Trafickking Humans

A brief description:
Path helps those who are taken into the sex-industry. They do this by providing a safe environment where the people who were abused may heal.
Here are a few statistics:

  1. About 100,000 - 300,000 children are currently being sold for sex in the U.S (according to the Polaris Project and MD Rescue & Restore Coalition)
  2. A 2011 prostitution sting operation in Little Rock revealed a few things:
    1. 50% were actively being trafficked or in prostitution as a direct result of being trafficked at an earlier age
    2. 85% of those that PATH has served were born and raised in Arkansas
